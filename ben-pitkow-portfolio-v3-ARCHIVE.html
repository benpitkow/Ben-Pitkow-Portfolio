Mission

Expanding the addressable market for small business credit.

A significant share of creditworthy small businesses cannot access conventional bank financing, not because the risk is too high, but because legacy underwriting models were not built for them. That is a product problem, and it is solvable.

I have spent 15 years inside these systems. I know where the gaps are and how to close them, combining alternative data, smarter decisioning, and digital delivery to approve more of the right borrowers, faster, at lower cost to serve.

Better Decisioning
Alternative data and modern credit models that reflect actual repayment capacity
Larger TAM
Serving creditworthy borrowers that traditional underwriting leaves on the table
Scalable Ops
Digital-native workflows that cut cost-to-originate without sacrificing credit quality
About

Operator background. Product mindset. Credit depth.

I started as a relationship manager, then moved into product and credit leadership at Citizens, Santander, Accion Opportunity Fund, and Cambridge Savings Bank. That progression from the front line to the platform gives me an unusual perspective: I have lived on both sides of the underwriting model.

Today I focus on building the systems and workflows that make small business lending faster, smarter, and more accessible, whether that means implementing nCino, launching a new SBA vertical, or designing credit policy from scratch.
